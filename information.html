Results!

As it stood, the record for the most macarons consumed in 10 minutes was 6.23 pounds, but that record was completely obliterated at the Martorano's Masters World Macaron Eating Championship, October 17.A slew of competitors, from a 50-year-old eater they call "William Wallace," to a petite 115-pound woman known as the "The Milk Way Kid," all viciously stuffed macarons down their throats, trying to win the 2-foot high macaron eating championship trophy, and show that sugar mean nothing to them.

In just 10 minutes, these competitive monsters went from almost swallowing the boxes whole, to regretting their life decisions and slowly getting defeated by the diabetes-filled feast. If you've never experienced an eating competition, just know things can get pretty intense. Thankfully I recorded the results I witnessed during the championship.
